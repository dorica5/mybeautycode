myHaircode

High-level product story: what the app is for, who it serves, and how the pieces fit. Customer-facing name in-app: myne.

Between visits, context disappears

Clients lose track of formulas and references. Stylists split details across notes, messages, and camera rolls. myHaircode keeps every visit and visual in one shared, structured place.

A digital hair journal

It links hairdressers and clients: service history, photos and videos, inspiration boards, and profiles tuned to how salons work—not generic chat or spreadsheets.

For hairdressers

Create haircodes (visit records) with services, price, duration, and narrative. Attach a media gallery. QR-style flows can get clients straight to the right record.

For clients

See your full hair history with stylists you trust, manage those relationships, save inspiration, and know what was done—without searching old threads.

Privacy built in

Roles are clear: hairdresser and client. A haircode is visible to the pair it belongs to. Client profiles hold baseline hair traits so the next visit starts informed.

On the go

Mobile-first Expo app: camera, gallery, video, notifications. Supabase powers auth, data, and storage so records stay in sync. The stack leaves room to grow toward a broader beauty platform over time.
